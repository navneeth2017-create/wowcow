How Your Commission Works

Your Store Commission

You earn 10% of monthly revenue from every store you're assigned to. This is your primary income stream.

Sponsor Bonus

When you enroll a rep, you earn 5% of their commission (not their revenue). This only applies to reps you directly enrolled — not reps they enroll.
